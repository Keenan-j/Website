Modern Hip-Hop

Why did rock lose it's spot at the top?

Hip hop became the most popular genre for the first time in history in 2018, marking a cultural shift of generations.

Dave Grohl of the Foo Fighters had chimed in on this shift stating that the latest generation of hip hop artists are the new rock stars of music, he felt they embody a lot of the old spirit of the rockstars of the 70's to 90's in the way they live life on the edge.

Another aspect of the new rockstars is the act of rebelion, straying from traditional hip hop and experimenting with new sounds and implimenting darker lyrics.
